# Repuestos — Category Campaign Strategy

**Status at launch: UNDER UMBRELLA.** Thin catalog (3 lot-based SKUs) BUT a distinct standing page exists: `/repuestos` — **"Motores de origen japonés."** ("Catálogo directo de Japón. Especificaciones técnicas verificadas por marca y modelo." — page-verbatim). This is the only category with a Japan-first sourcing story.

**Important framing (assumption A8, recon doc):** repuestos is a B2B lot business — "Caja x 50 unidades" (`seed.json`, filtro de aceite) — NOT retail parts. Ads target purchasing roles, not end consumers. This also makes it the natural A4/A5 recruitment category: parts buyers repeat monthly, making them the highest-LTV lead type on the platform.

## Pain points & hook angles (real product data)

1. **Counterfeit fatigue (A1/A4):** the Peruvian parts market is flooded with unverifiable product; "especificaciones técnicas verificadas por marca y modelo" + certificate-of-origin discipline is the counter. Japan-origin engine story amplifies it.
2. **Lot economics (A4):** caja x 50, compra por lote — the reseller wants unit economics per box, which is exactly Mister's MOQ table surface (lane A4 `MOQ` node).
3. **Fleet uptime (A1):** kit de embrague 70–120 HP matched to the tractor range Wings itself sells — cross-sell hook to machinery buyers ("el repuesto de tu tractor viaja por el mismo corredor").
4. **Recurring corridor (A4/A5):** parts repeat; a recurring lane is Mister lane A3/A5 vocabulary — program framing for the biggest buyers.

## Targeting (on activation)
- A4 cell: Venta al por mayor + Repuestos/Autopartes + Comercio interests; geo commercial hubs (Lima, Arequipa, Trujillo, Juliaca).
- A1 cell: Gestión de flotas/Transporte + Agricultura interests (fleet + farm maintenance).
- Retargeting cross-sell: R1 audience of `/catalogo/maquinaria-agricola` + `/catalogo/camiones` visitors gets RP3.
- Optimization `Lead`; destinations `/repuestos` and product pages.
