# Buses — Category Campaign Strategy

**Status at launch: UNDER UMBRELLA** (inventory: 3–4 SKUs — Bus Urbano 12m, Interurbano 13m, Minibús 7m; below the 8-SKU breakout bar in `02-brand-campaign/strategy.md` §4). This kit pre-builds the standalone campaign; until breakout, concepts BU1–BU3 rotate inside the umbrella and R1 retargeting.

**Breakout trigger to watch:** transport-company demand is tender-driven (concesiones municipales, renovación de rutas, transporte de personal minero). One tender season or one ops-reported fleet inquiry justifies activation regardless of the lead-count trigger.

## Pain points & hook angles (from real product data, `seed.json`)

1. **Capacity economics (A2):** Bus Urbano 12m = 90 pasajeros, piso bajo, accesibilidad universal, Euro V — the exact spec vocabulary of municipal concession requirements. Hook = compliance-readiness, not comfort.
2. **Route revenue (A1/A2):** Interurbano 13m = 49 asientos reclinables + bodega 9 m³ — seats and cargo hold are revenue lines; frame the spec as route arithmetic.
3. **Personnel transport (A2):** Minibús 7m, 25 pasajeros, "transporte de personal y turismo" (site-verbatim) — mining/agro crew transport contracts are a quietly large Peruvian niche.
4. **Fleet procurement discipline:** buses are bought against contracts with dates → documentation promise carries the close.

## Targeting (on activation)
- A2 cell: Transporte público + Autobús + Gestión de flotas (if available) + Turismo interests, page-admin behavior; geo Lima, Arequipa, Cusco (turismo), mining depts for personnel transport.
- A1 cell: Turismo + Transporte interests, provinces with interprovincial route culture (Puno, Cusco, Junín).
- Optimization: `Lead`; destination product detail pages.
