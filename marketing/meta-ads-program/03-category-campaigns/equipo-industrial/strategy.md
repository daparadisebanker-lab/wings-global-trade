# Equipo Industrial — Category Campaign Strategy

**Status at launch: UNDER UMBRELLA** (3 SKUs: Generador Diésel 250 kVA, Montacargas 3 t, Compresor de Tornillo 50 HP — below breakout bar). Kit pre-built; concepts rotate in umbrella + R1.

## Archetype mapping

| Archetype | Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| A1 Lead/End Buyer | **Primary.** Dueño de planta/taller/almacén PYME comprando un activo puntual | Generator and compressor are classic owner purchases |
| A2 Project Manager | **Primary.** Obra/planta/faena que necesita energía, aire o manipulación dentro de un cronograma | Spec + certificates register |
| A3 Logistics | Marginal — montacargas para depósitos; reached via Mister | |

## Pain points & hook angles (real product data)

1. **Continuity anxiety (A1):** a generator is bought against the memory of the last blackout — lost production, spoiled cold chain. Generador 250 kVA has transferencia automática — the spec that means "you don't even get up." Hook = continuity, proven by the transfer spec.
2. **Noise/compliance (A2):** cabina insonorizada 75 dB, IP54 on the compressor — urban-industrial compliance details that procurement actually checks.
3. **Operating cost realism (A1):** consumo 48 L/h published openly — publishing the consumption figure IS the trust move (brand rule: never claim quality, show data).
4. **Warehouse throughput (A1/A3):** montacargas 3 t, torre 3 m, desplazador lateral — patio + almacén versatility.
