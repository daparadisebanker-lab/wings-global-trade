# Brand Strategist Contribution — Wings Global Trade

## Visual Thesis

**Trade intelligence visualized at the precision of a customs manifest, with the warmth of a handshake.**

Every visual decision derives from this: documentation-grade precision (DM Mono, exact numbers, tight grids) + human proximity (warm paper tones, serif headlines, real market names).

---

## Category Design

Wings defines a new category: **The Digital Trade Operator.**

Not a marketplace (no transactions). Not a directory (it has AI). Not a broker (it's a platform). Wings is the first B2B trade platform in LATAM with a managed free zone AI engine embedded in the discovery layer. The category name for positioning: *Operador Comercial Digital*.

Competitors don't exist in this category because they chose either "marketplace" or "consulting" — Wings does the digital work of both.

## Brand Positioning Stack

| Layer | Definition |
|---|---|
| **WHO** | LATAM resellers, distributors, and SME importers who import at volume and need a trusted partner with free zone access |

## Voice and Tone

**Three words:** Direct. Operational. Earned.

**Five things Wings never says:**
1. "Soluciones" (too generic)
2. "De alta calidad" (empty claim)
3. Exclamation marks — ever
4. First-person plural corporate speak: "Estamos comprometidos con..."
5. Buzzwords: "innovador," "disruptivo," "ecosistema"

**Register:** Peer-to-peer. Senior buyer to senior trade partner. Not salesy. Never condescending.

**Vocabulary Wings owns:**
- Precisión / Proximidad / Confianza (tagline triad)
- "Zona franca" — stated with operational authority, not explanation
- "FOB · CIF · HS" — used naturally, not defined (the audience knows)
- "Motor Accio" — proprietary name for the AI flow
- "Consulta técnica" not "formulario" — it's a brief, not a form

---

## The ONE Ownable Thing

**Wings publishes the CIF estimate before you call them.**

No competitor does this. Every freight forwarder, importer, and trade broker in LATAM makes you call before getting a number. Wings shows you FOB + freight + insurance + duty — live, in the session. This single behavior communicates operational reality better than any copy claim.

**Implication for build:** The CIF estimate card must feel like a Bloomberg terminal, not a price quote widget. Every number justified. Source market explicit. Free zone named.

---

## Thought Leadership Angle

**"El margen se pierde entre el proveedor y el puerto — no en la fábrica."**

Wings believes that LATAM importers are losing 15–40% of their margin in the logistics and customs corridor — not in sourcing. The free zone is a structural advantage most importers don't know they can access. Wings' positioning is not "we have better products" but "we know the corridor better than anyone."

This should manifest as: educational content, Accio Engine's CIF breakdown, and the way the platform explains ZOFRATACNA and ZOFRI.
